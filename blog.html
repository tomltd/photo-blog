Colorado 2016

22st June

Woke up at 5:30 this morning and could sleep no more so we got up and had a bit of breakfast and headed out downtown. Wandering about the Capitol building it was very quiet apart from a few squirrels and the odd homeless person.

No where was open so we went for a coffee and a wizz to kill some time until the Colorado History Museum opened at 10. There was an interesting exhibit about mining in the Rockies with a fake lift and BLASTING game.

Took the free bus up 16th and hit Biker Jims Gourmet Hot Dogs. Sampled some dogs and craft beer then went for a second beer at the Falling Rock Tap House.

Wandered up to Union Station and went for another wizz then dunked our feet in the river at confluence park. I may live to regret that as the water was pretty trashy.

Had a quick look around REI then got the bus back and walked back to the apartment for burritos and salad
